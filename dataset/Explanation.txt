
This research utilizes the World University Rankings 2023 dataset comprising 2,341 institutions worldwide. The dataset contains 13 variables including University Rank, Name, Location, student numbers, staff ratios, international student percentages, gender ratios, and multiple performance scores. Key variables for this analysis include Overall Score (measuring comprehensive institutional performance), Teaching Score, Research Score, Citations Score, Industry Income Score, and International Outlook Score. The dataset provides a comprehensive snapshot of global higher education performance across multiple dimensions, enabling robust comparative analysis between countries.
